Instructions

1. Navigate to Order History Reports.

Visit Amazon's Order History Report. Login if needed.

For the Report Type option, verify that Items is selected.

For the Start Date select January 1, 2022. For End Date click the Use Today.

2. Request your data.

Click the Request Report button to begin exporting your order data.

Leave the browser window open and monitor the progress of the report creation in the Your Reports table. Once the page refreshes when the report is complete, click the Download link to retrieve the file of your order history.

Note that if your report generation is taking an extended amount of time, you may close your browser window, as Amazon will e-mail you a download link to retrieve the file as well.

Once you have downloaded your file, proceed to the next page to upload your data to the study.
